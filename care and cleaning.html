CARE AND CLEANING

Keeping your DEA Cup clean is essential for your vaginal health as well as for prolonging the cup’s life. During your period, wash your cup with mild soap and warm water whenever emptying it.

Inspect your cup often – if you notice any surface damage, please replace your cup immediately.

BEFORE USING YOUR DEA CUP FOR THE FIRST TIME

2.Practice folding your DEA Cup

3.Read and follow the insertion / removal instructions
